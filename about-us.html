Our Commitment to Nepalese Cinema and Culture

With over ten years of experience in the industry, Namaste Entertainment is dedicated to the distribution and production of captivating Nepalese movies and events. Our mission is to connect the Nepalese community living overseas, particularly in Australia and New Zealand with their cultural heritage through cinema. We do movie distribution directly to the cinema's chains to make it more efficient and affordable to our audience and showcase and build Nepali movie audience around. We are committed to bring the best movie experience to you at a glance.

Bridging Cultures Through Film

At Namaste Entertainment, we believe in the power of storytelling to transcend borders. We strive to bring authentic Nepalese narratives to local Australian theatres, creating a space where our community can celebrate their culture and connect with their roots.

A Vision for the Future

As we continue to grow, our focus remains on serving the Nepalese community. While currently operating independently, we aspire to expand our team and enhance our offerings to better serve our audience.

Namaste Entertainment

At Namaste Entertainment, we bring the vibrant world of Nepalese cinema and cultural events to audiences across Australia. As a premier movie and event distribution company, we provide a seamless platform to explore, book, and enjoy the latest cinematic releases and exclusive cultural experiences.

  • Movies

    Our carefully curated selection of Nepalese movies showcases a range of genres, from heartfelt dramas to thrilling adventures. Discover the best of Nepalese cinema and stay updated on the latest releases.

  • Event

    Namaste Entertainment offers exclusive cultural events, film screenings, and community gatherings. Our events celebrate the richness of Nepalese heritage and bring people together to experience the essence of Nepal.

  • Distribution

    We handle all aspects of movie/events distribution, from marketing to ticketing, ensuring audiences have easy access to Nepalese films across Australia.
